Complex QAM Modulation and Demodulation

Quadrature Amplitude Modulation (QAM) is a modulation scheme that combines amplitude and phase modulation by transmitting two signals (in-phase and quadrature components) on the same carrier frequency. Complex QAM allows efficient use of bandwidth and is widely used in communication systems such as Wi-Fi, LTE, and digital TV.
