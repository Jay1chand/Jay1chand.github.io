QPSK Simulation

This tool simulates Quadrature Phase Shift Keying (QPSK), a digital modulation scheme used in modern communication systems. You can specify the number of symbols to transmit, the noise power for the Additive White Gaussian Noise (AWGN) channel, and phase noise strength. The simulation generates constellation and signal plots to visualize how noise and distortions affect the QPSK-modulated signal.
